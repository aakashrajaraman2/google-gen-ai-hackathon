## Comparison Report of Insurance Policies:

This report compares two insurance policies: 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' and 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012'.

**Contract Title:** 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' has a more specific and descriptive contract title compared to 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012'.

**Parties Involved:** The 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' policy involves multiple parties: Mobil in Deutschland e.V. (MiD), Automobilclub von Deutschland e.V. (AvD e.V.), Helvetia Schweizerische Versicherungsgesellschaft in Liechtenstein AG, and mobile GARANTIE Deutschland GmbH.  The 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' policy only mentions KS Versicherungs-AG as the involved party.

**Insurance Provider:** 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' lists Helvetia Schweizerische Versicherungsgesellschaft in Liechtenstein AG as the primary insurance provider, while 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' directly identifies KS Versicherungs-AG as the insurance provider. 

**Legal Support:** Both policies lack information regarding legal support provided. 

**Insured Persons:** 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' specifies insured persons as members of Mobil in Deutschland e.V. with a basic membership. Conversely, 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' defines insured persons as the policyholder and all occupants of the insured vehicles. 

**Primary Member:** The 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' policy does not explicitly define a primary member. However, the 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' policy identifies the policyholder as the primary member.

**Member Responsibilities:** 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' details specific member responsibilities, including reporting damages, minimizing losses, and providing documentation. In contrast, 'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' lacks information regarding member responsibilities.

**Summary:** 'Versicherungsbedingungen MiD-Basis-Mitgliedschaft' provides a more comprehensive overview of the policy's details, including multiple parties involved, specific insured persons, and clearly defined member responsibilities.  'KS-Schutzbrief 2012' presents a simpler overview, focusing primarily on the insurance provider and insured persons. Both policies lack information regarding legal support.
